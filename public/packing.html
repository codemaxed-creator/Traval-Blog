Packing Essentials

Everything you need to pack smarter and travel easier.

✔ Smart Packing Tips

  • Always roll your clothes to save space.
  • Use packing cubes to organize items.
  • Keep your important items in your carry-on.
  • Pack multi-purpose clothes that you can mix and match.

✔ Must-Have Items

  • Travel-size toiletries
  • Portable charger (Power bank)
  • First aid kit
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Small locks and zip pouches

✔ Long Trip Essentials

  • Extra clothing layers
  • Weather-appropriate gear (raincoat, warm jacket)
  • Travel laundry bag
  • Backup mobile phone / SIM
